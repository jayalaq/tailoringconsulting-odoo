This module is allow to pay with multiple currencies from POS Interface.

In the configuration of each point of sale you can enable collection in multiple currencies.

* The images are referential, because it may vary from one version to another.

When making the Collection, you can activate the option to charge in foreign currency


* The images are referential, because it may vary from one version to another.

1. You choose the payment method.

2. You activate the collection in foreign currency.

3. You choose the foreign currency.

4. You verify the Conversion Ratio that the system shows you, which has been loaded from the Odoo rate registry for each currency.

5. Only if necessary, the rate to be used can be updated.

6. Enter the amount that you are giving us in foreign currency.

Odoo will calculate the amount in local currency and tell you what the redelivery amount is in local currency.

You can print the receipt in foreign currency.


* The images are referential, because it may vary from one version to another.
